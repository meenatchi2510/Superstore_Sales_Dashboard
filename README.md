# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling
**Internship:** Data Analyst Internship - ElevateLabs

## Objective
Create visualizations that convey a compelling business story using the Superstore sales dataset.

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop

## Dataset
Sample - Superstore dataset (Sales, Profit, Discount, Category, Sub-Category, Region, Segment, Order Date, etc.)

## Dashboard Overview

### Page 1: Sales & Profit Dashboard
1. **Sum of Sales by Sub-Category** – Horizontal bar chart showing Phones, Chairs, and Storage as top revenue-generating sub-categories.
2. **Sum of Sales and Sum of Profit by Category** – Clustered column chart comparing sales and profit across Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies.
3. **Sum of Discount and Sum of Profit by Sub-Category** – Scatter plot showing the relationship between discount levels and profitability.
4. **Sum of Sales by Segment** – Pie chart showing sales distribution across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.

### Page 2: Key Business Insights
A summary page highlighting the main takeaways from the dashboard in plain business language.

## Key Insights
- Phones, Chairs, and Storage are the top 3 revenue-generating sub-categories.
- Furniture category shows high sales but disproportionately low profit, indicating a possible pricing or cost issue.
- Technology delivers the strongest profit margin among all categories despite moderate sales.
- Consumer segment contributes the largest share of total sales (~50%), followed by Corporate and Home Office.
- Higher discount levels show a visible negative impact on profit across multiple sub-categories.
- **Recommendation:** Review discount strategy on low-margin sub-categories to improve overall profitability.
